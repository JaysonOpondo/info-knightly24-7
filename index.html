About Us

Knightly Twenty Four Seven Limited is a company specializing in the provision of Clearing and Forwarding services to traders within the East African Region. The company is licensed by the Customs and Excise Department of Kenya Revenue Authority to operate as a clearing agent and provide clearing and forwarding services. The company is also a member of the Kenya International Freight and Warehousing Association (KIFWA) vide membership number M2641.

The company's headquaters is in Mombasa - the gateway into Kenya and to the East African region as 90% of all cargo comes into the region through the Port of Mombasa. To effectively service the clients based in Nairobi, the company also operates a branch within the capital.

Clearing and Forwarding is our core service and we understand the importance of timely delivery. For timely and professionl clearing and forwarding services, Knightly Twenty Four Seven has been a very reliable and dependable company to it's clients.

Since it's inception in 2021, the company envisages being a market leader in the provision of import and export solutions.The services are being offered at very competitive rates to all our clients.

The company enjoys customer loyalty and confidence due to its uncompromised adherence to the law, intergrity and honesty in its dealingswith all its clients and agents appointed by its clients.

Our Services

Our main business is the provision of clearing and forwarding services to both importers and exporters. Services offered include:

  • Customs clearing
  • International Forwarding
  • Warehousing arrangement
  • Export packing
  • Import unpacking
  • Customs examination

We have no restrictions in the size and weight of the goods we handle, and we provide a full array of freight forwarding services like door-to-door pick up and managing shipping documents. As per the requirement of the customer we offer FCL load service and LCL service with ease, and clear visibility within the region. We also pre-arrange the shipment entries to the concerned destination before to the vessels arrival to provide timely release of the goods after customs clearance. Quotation on cost of freight, port charges, consular fees, and cost of special documentation, insurance and fee of the freight forwarder are also provided to the clients. We pride ourselves in providing comprehensive services through major regional border and custom entry points.
